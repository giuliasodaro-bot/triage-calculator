Setting Call Script

Frame
"Hi [Name], it's [Your Name] from Elevated Recovery, can you hear me okay?
I am one of the Reboot Strategists on JK’s team.
Did I catch you at a bad time? ... private conversation?"
Situation
How long has this been part of your life? How often do you find yourself going to it?
When was the last time you went to it? What usually triggers it?
Gap
How has your life been impacted by this — especially your relationship, work, energy, or confidence?
What price have you already paid because of this?
What do you think continues to happen if nothing changes in the next 6–12 months?
Missing / Ideal
What have you been doing to end this behavior?
Ideally, what would your life look like once this is truly behind you?
What do you feel you need to resolve this once and for all?
Urgency
You've been dealing with this for X time... so is resolving it a later or a sooner thing for you?
Recap + Qualifier
Just to make sure I understand... [reflect pain]. Is that accurate?
Tell me about what you do for work... (income + decision power)
Final Fit: "Does that describe where you're at right now?"

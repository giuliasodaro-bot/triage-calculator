Elevated Recovery - Reboot Strategist Tools

Setting Call Script

Frame
"Hi [Name], it's [Your Name] from Elevated Recovery, can you hear me okay?
I am one of the Reboot Strategists on JK’s team.
Did I catch you at a bad time?
Just so you know, everything you share is private and confidential.
I had about 10 minutes before my next appointment and I thought I would try giving you a call to see if we can help you in any way with your out of control behavior.
Are you in a place where you can have a private conversation?"
Situation
How long has this been part of your life?
Has it evolved over time?
Over the past 3/6 months... How often do you find yourself going to it?
"When was the last time you went to it?"
What usually triggers it?
Gap
How has your life been impacted by this — especially your relationship, work, energy, or confidence?
What price have you already paid because of this?
What do you think continues to happen if nothing changes in the next 6–12 months?
Missing / Ideal
What have you been doing to end this behavior and reboot your life?
What do you feel is still missing for you to actually solve this once and for all?
"Ideally, what would your life look like once this is truly behind you?"
What do you feel you need in order to resolve the situation once and for all?
Urgency
If you don’t mind me asking, you have been dealing with this already for X time...
so is resolving it a later or a sooner thing for you?
Recap + Qualifier
Just to make sure I understand... the main things hurting right now are [reflect their words: marriage at risk, feeling out of control at work, secrecy/guilt, etc.]. Is that accurate?
"Tell me about what you do for work or business right now?" (Listen for income level & decision-making power)
"Are you married or in a committed relationship?"
"Does your partner know about this, or is it still private?"
"Are you in a position to make a decision on getting help yourself, or would you need to involve your wife/partner?"
Final Fit Question: "From what you’ve shared, it sounds like you’re at a point where [briefly summarize their level/pain]. We specialize in helping men who are serious about investing in a real solution because they know they can’t do it alone anymore. Does that describe where you’re at right now?"
